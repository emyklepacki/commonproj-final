(1) Spicy Fried Chicken Sandwich Recipe

Dish: Spicy Chicken Sandwich

Origin: American

Source: Original recipe!

Category: Main Dish


Recipe Ingredients:

  • Chicken Prep:
  • 1.5lb chicken thigh
  • 2 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tbsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp black pepper
  • dash/pinch of salt
  • 3 cups of flour
  • 3 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp hot sauce
  • Marinade
  • 1 cup preferred hot sauce
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • dash salt
  • 4 tbsp melted butter
  • Bun/toppings
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • crinkled pickles
  • tomatoes
  • lettuce
  • Chipotle Mayo

Recipe Steps:

  1. Wash your chicken and leave it to rest in a bowl
  2. Begin working on your marinade. In a medium/large bowl, combine your hot sauce, eggs, black pepper, butter, and salt one-by-one. Mix until fully dissolved
  3. In a separate bowl, combine flour, garlic powder, paprika, cumin, black pepper, salt together. Whisk thoroughly.
  4. Prep your chicken thigh by poking holes with a fork across its surface on both sides. Place your chicken into the marinade mix and let rest for 1-2h. (if speed is needed, can do a quick soak of 10-15min)
  5. Place your marinated chicken into flour mixture and toss. Place back in marinade and redcoat. Repeat 2-3 times.
  6. Prepare your vegetables and set them aside.
  7. Grab a deep pan and add your oil on medium-high heat. Fry in oil for 4-5min on each side.
  8. Remove chicken thigh and toss in 1tbsp hot sauce before plating.
  9. Combine the buns, toppings, and chicken and serve!

(2) Tomato and Eggs

Origin: China

Source: Own Recipe

Category: Main Dish


Recipe Ingredients:

  • Tomatoes
  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Sugar
  • Cooking Oil

Recipe Steps:

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl and mix until scrambled.
  2. Cut tomatoes into pieces around the size of your thumb.
  3. Oil a hot pan and cook the eggs until satisfied then remove the eggs.
  4. Re-oil the pan and cook the tomatoes until they loosen.
  5. Combine eggs and tomatoes and season with salt and sugar.

(3) Shrimp Scampi

Origin: Italy

Source: New York Times

Category: Main Dish


Recipe Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • ½ cup dry white wine or broth
  • ¾ teaspoon kosher salt, or to taste
  • ⅛ teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes, or to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • 1¾ pounds large or extra-large shrimp, shelled
  • ⅓ cup chopped parsley
  • Freshly squeezed juice of half a lemon/li>
  • Cooked pasta or crusty bread

Recipe Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, melt butter with olive oil.
  2. Add garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  3. Add wine or broth, salt, red pepper flakes and plenty of black pepper and bring to a simmer.
  4. Let the wine reduce by half, about 2 minutes.
  5. Add shrimp and sauté until they just turn pink, 2 to 4 minutes depending upon their size.
  6. Stir in the parsley and lemon juice and serve over pasta or accompanied by crusty bread.
